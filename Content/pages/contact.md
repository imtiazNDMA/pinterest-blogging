## A quick note

We are a small-space styling site, not a licensed horticulturist, contractor, or legal advisor. Our guides share general ideas and what to look for. For plant safety around pets, always check the [ASPCA toxic and non-toxic plant list](https://www.aspca.org/pet-care/animal-poison-control/toxic-and-non-toxic-plants), and for anything structural (mounting, drilling, wind load, or your lease and HOA rules) confirm with the manufacturer or your building before you build.
