## ⚠ Safety / YMYL — hedge these (non-negotiable)

- **Freestanding screens & panels:** flag that lightweight panels **catch wind** — weight the base or anchor; defer to manufacturer load/anchoring specs. Don't promise they'll stay put in a storm.
- **Lattice / trellis / vertical screens:** anchor against wind; weight/watering load when planted. Defer to manufacturer.
- **Pergola / overhead / shade sail:** larger/permanent structures = **wind-load + anchoring + possible permits**. Defer to **manufacturer instructions + local building code**. Not engineering advice.
- **Privacy walls & fences:** **check the lease / HOA before fixing or building anything**, especially fences and walls. **Property-line / setback rules apply** — defer to local rules; **this is not legal advice**. Get the boundary right before building.
- **Privacy plants:** name plants **generically**; hedge **light needs (sun/part-shade/shade) and hardiness/zone** only; defer detailed care to the patio garden post. **Any pet-safe / toxic claim REQUIRES an ASPCA citation** (https://www.aspca.org/pet-care/animal-poison-control/toxic-and-non-toxic-plants) — never from memory.
- **Bamboo:** recommend **clumping or fully contained** bamboo only; note running bamboo is **invasive** — hedge.
- **No fabricated experience** ("I built this / this survived a storm"). Lived claims go in `[PERSONAL TIP]` for the human to fill.
