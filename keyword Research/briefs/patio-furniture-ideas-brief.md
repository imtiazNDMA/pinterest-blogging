## YMYL / safety flags (verify, do not fabricate)
- **Wind & stability:** light furniture (aluminum, resin, foldables) can blow over or off an elevated patio — advise anchoring, weighting, or storing in wind. State as guidance, not a tested claim.
- **Umbrella / pergola / overhead structure wind-load:** defer to manufacturer specs and local conditions — do not give load numbers.
- **Lease / HOA:** check before fixing or bolting anything (anchors, wall mounts) on a rental or HOA patio.
- **Cushion fire-safety:** mention only if a fire pit / heater is referenced — otherwise omit. No durability-test or longevity claims that imply we tested products.
