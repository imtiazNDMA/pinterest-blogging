# Master Strategy

## 1. Content pillars
1. **Container & balcony how-tos** — e.g. "best vegetables for balcony pots," "vertical gardening for small balconies"
2. **Plant care by problem/plant** — e.g. "why are my container tomatoes dying," "herbs that grow in shade"
3. **Gear & setup** (money posts) — e.g. "best self-watering planters," "grow lights for apartments"
4. **Inspiration / ideas** (Pinterest fuel) — e.g. "small balcony garden ideas," "renter-friendly garden setups"

Boards + topic clusters mirror these four pillars.

## 2. Monetization ladder
| Stage | Traffic | Monetization |
|---|---|---|
| Launch → ~Month 3 | 0–1k sessions | Amazon Associates (day 1) + garden affiliate programs |
| ~1k sessions | 1k+/mo | Apply to **Mediavine Journey** immediately |
| Scaling | 25k+ pageviews | Consider **Raptive**; compare RPM vs Journey |
| Mature | 50k+ | Premium tier negotiation + own digital products (printables, e-book) |

**Affiliate mix:** Amazon (breadth, 3% but everything-in-cart for 24h) + higher-rate garden programs (seed companies, planter/tool brands, ~5–15%) + eventually own printable garden planners.

## 3. Tech setup (bootstrap, <$30/mo)
- **Hosting:** budget managed WordPress host (~$3–7/mo intro) + domain (~$10/yr).
- **Theme:** free fast theme (Kadence / GeneratePress free) — speed = SEO + ad RPM.
- **Free plugins:** SEO (Rank Math free), caching, image optimization, pin-it/social.
- **Pinterest:** Business account (free) + claim website + enable Rich Pins.

## 4. Keyword research loop (free tools)
1. Pinterest search-bar autocomplete + Pinterest Trends (what Pinterest users search).
2. Cross-check Google autocomplete, "People Also Ask," related searches.
3. Validate with Google Keyword Planner (free) + free tiers of keyword tools.
4. Log clusters in the `keyword Research` folder (1 pillar → many long-tail posts).

## 5. Content strategy
- **Structure:** topic clusters — a few pillar guides + many long-tail supporting posts, interlinked.
- **Mix:** ~70% informational (ad RPM) / ~30% buyer "best X" posts (affiliate).
- **SEO:** one long-tail keyword per post; fully satisfy intent; original photos + real experience (E-E-A-T); strong internal linking.
- **AI use:** draft/outline with AI, but add genuine experience, photos, and editing — thin generic AI content gets penalized; lived experience is the moat.
- **Cadence:** 3–5 quality posts/week.

## 6. Pinterest strategy
- **Boards:** keyword-named, one per pillar + sub-topic boards.
- **Pins:** vertical **2:3** (1000×1500), bold text overlay, keyword-rich titles + descriptions; **multiple fresh pin designs per post.**
- **Cadence:** ~5–10 fresh pins/day, scheduled and spaced (native scheduler or free Tailwind tier).
- **Idea/video pins:** a few per week for algorithm trust (not clickable).
- **Golden rule:** fresh pins win — constant new images/designs, not repinning the same one.

## 7. Weekly rhythm (~20 hrs)
- ~8 hrs writing/editing (3–5 posts)
- ~5 hrs pin design + scheduling
- ~3 hrs keyword/niche research + planning
- ~2 hrs Pinterest engagement + analytics
- ~2 hrs learning/optimization

## 8. Milestones & KPIs
- **Month 1:** site live, 10–15 posts, Pinterest account + boards, daily pinning.
- **Month 2–3:** 30–50 posts, apply to Mediavine Journey at 1k sessions, first Amazon commissions.
- **Month 4–6:** 80–120 posts, growing Pinterest sessions, optimize top performers.
- **Month 6–12:** push to 25k+ pageviews, evaluate Raptive, add digital products.
- **Track:** Pinterest impressions → outbound clicks, GA4 sessions, top posts, RPM, affiliate EPC.

## 9. Risks & mitigations
- **Pinterest dependence** → build every post as a Google SEO asset too, not just pin bait.
- **Thin AI content penalty** → always inject real experience + original photos.
- **Slow-start discouragement** → the 1k-session Mediavine threshold gives an early monetization win.

**Core principle:** every post is a DUAL-PURPOSE asset — it satisfies Google search intent (durable, compounding traffic) AND spawns multiple fresh Pinterest pins (fast traffic). Two compounding channels from the same work.

# Reference: 2026 monetization facts
_Verified via web search on 2026-06-24 — re-check before relying; these change._

- **Mediavine Journey:** accepts at **1,000 sessions/month** (since Jan 15, 2026), 70% rev share, requires Grow plugin; auto-upgrades to Official after $5,000 ad revenue in trailing 12 months.
- **Raptive:** **25,000 monthly pageviews** (down from 100k); 25k–99,999 band needs 50% tier-1 traffic (US/UK/CA/AU/NZ).
- **Amazon Associates:** Outdoor/Lawn & Garden = **3%** (cut from 8% in 2020). Mitigate with volume + 24h cart attribution + higher-rate garden affiliate programs.
- **Pinterest 2026:** prioritizes FRESH pins; vertical 2:3 only; 5–15 pins/day; keyword everything (titles, descriptions, board names, filenames); entity/topical consistency builds authority; idea/video pins build trust; ~8–11 PM audience timezone is a strong window.
