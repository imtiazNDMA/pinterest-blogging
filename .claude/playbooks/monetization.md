# Playbook: Monetization (Affiliate Ladder + Ads)

Used by **monetization-strategist** / `/monetize-audit`. Stage monetization so quality is never sacrificed.
All rules in `guardrails/affiliate-compliance.md` are mandatory. Re-verify rates/thresholds — they change.

## The ladder (from `.claude/memory.md`)
| Stage | Traffic | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Launch → ~Mo 3 | 0–1k sessions | Amazon Associates (apply after ~10 strong posts) + garden affiliate programs |
| ~1k sessions/mo | 1k+ | Apply to **Mediavine Journey** (Grow plugin, 70% rev share) |
| Scaling | 25k+ pageviews | Evaluate **Raptive**; compare RPM vs Journey |
| Mature | 50k+ | Premium negotiation + own digital products (printable planners, e-book) |

## Affiliate mix
- **Amazon Associates** — breadth + 24h whole-cart attribution (earns on anything bought), but only ~3% on garden.
  Requires 3 qualified sales within 180 days of approval to keep the account.
- **Higher-rate garden programs** (~5–15%) — seed companies, planter/tool brands. Prefer these for the actual
  product pick when quality is equal; keep Amazon as the convenient fallback link.
- Maintain `Growth Strategy/affiliate-programs.md`: program, rate, cookie window, signup link, best-fit products.

## Buyer-post product selection (the money posts)
1. State **selection criteria first** (what makes a good one for THIS reader), then the picks.
2. Per pick: what it is, who it's for, key features, trade-offs, qualitative value. **NO prices/stock/"cheapest".**
3. Best affiliate source per product; descriptive anchor; disclosure above the first link.
4. Internal-link related buyer posts to each other + up to the cluster pillar.

## Application timing (don't apply too early)
- **Amazon:** ≥10 strong original posts, trust pages live, natural product recommendations, easy navigation.
- **AdSense:** original useful content, policy compliant, trust pages, not thin/unfinished.
- **Journey/Raptive:** hit the traffic threshold with clean tier-1 traffic + good reader experience.

## Ad-network readiness audit output
A gap checklist per network (content, pages, traffic, UX) + which milestone unlocks it →
`Growth Strategy/ad-network-readiness.md`.

## Scaling rule
Double down on posts/clusters that earn outbound clicks + commissions; add buyer posts where informational
posts already get traffic; keep informational content strong to sustain ad RPM.
