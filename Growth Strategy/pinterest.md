# Pinterest Roadmap — Bloom & Lantern

> The phased plan to turn Pinterest into the blog's primary traffic engine. Pinterest is a **visual search
> engine**, treated as SEO; the blog is where the clicks monetize.
> Companions: `Growth Strategy/setup-checklist.md` (the board taxonomy + account setup steps),
> `.claude/guardrails/pinterest-policy.md` (non-negotiable rules), vault `03 Pinterest/` notes.
> Brand: **Bloom & Lantern** / `https://bloomandlantern.com`. Last updated 2026-06-27.

---

## Operating model (the one idea that drives everything)
- **Board = a content pillar** (broad head keyword). **Pin = a focused long-tail keyword.**
- 12 pillar boards: 6 balcony (Decor / Furniture / Privacy / Garden / Flooring / Lighting) + 6 patio
  (Decor / Furniture / Garden / Privacy / Covered / Flooring). Full names + descriptions in
  `setup-checklist.md`.
- A well-fed pillar board is a strong topical-relevance signal; each pin inside stays narrowly keyworded and
  points to its post.
- **Fresh pins only**, strict **2:3 / 1000×1500**, **label AI** (home decor is on Pinterest's 2026 GenAI
  "see less" list), keyword everything (pin title, description, board, filename), no emojis, no hashtag spam.

## Phase 2 — Ramp & consistency *(months 2–3)*
- [ ] Increase to **5–10 fresh pins/day**, spaced; multiple distinct pins per post over time (new design each).
- [ ] A few **Idea pins / week** for algorithm trust (reach, not direct clicks).
- [ ] **Open the empty-pillar boards** as their content gets written (start the Balcony Furniture money pillar).
- [ ] Connect **Pinterest Analytics** (auto once the site is claimed); start tracking top pins + saves.

## Phase 3 — Optimize & scale *(months 3–6)*
- [ ] Feed the **weekly-review loop** (`/weekly-review`): find winning pins/boards, make **more fresh pins of winners**, retire flops.
- [ ] Join **2–3 vetted** balcony/small-space **group boards** (engagement over size).
- [ ] **Seasonal boards** ahead of season — Christmas / Fall / Winter balcony — pinned 6–8 weeks early.
- [ ] Refresh top posts; ensure internal links + new pin angles keep winners alive.

## Phase 4 — Monetization scale *(6–12 months)*
- [ ] At **1,000 sessions / 30 days → apply to Mediavine Journey** (ad income); keep scaling pins.
- [ ] Layer affiliate-driven pins (buyer posts: furniture, planters, lighting) once traffic is proven.
- [ ] Graduate toward Raptive (25k pageviews) as volume compounds.

## Cadence & KPIs
- **Cadence:** month 1 = 1–3 pins/day; warmed = 5–10/day; always spaced, evening slots.
- **Leading metrics:** pin impressions, saves, outbound clicks, pin CTR (log in vault [[Pin Tracker]] / tracker CSV).
- **Milestones:** 9 fed boards live → every post has a batch → 1k blog sessions/30d (Mediavine) → 25k pageviews (Raptive).
- **Quality bar (every pin):** 2:3, authentic image, no garbled text/logos/prices, on-brand, AI labeled, keyworded, matches destination.

## Responsibilities
- **Studio (Claude):** pin batches (copy + AI image prompts + board + schedule), board taxonomy, retargeting maps, weekly-review analysis.
- **Owner:** create boards, generate/QA pin images, schedule in Pinterest, publish, paste analytics exports back for the review loop.
