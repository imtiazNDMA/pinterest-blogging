## Reading the data (decisions, not vanity)
- **High impressions, low clicks** (pin) → redesign the pin (overlay/hook), not the post.
- **High clicks, low time-on-page** (post) → intent mismatch or weak intro; revise.
- **Rising Search Console query** → expand that post or spin a new one targeting it.
- **A post outperforming** → write 2–3 siblings + more pins on that angle.
- **A post flat after 3–4 months** → refresh (new pins, updated content, better internal links).
