## Metrics defined
- **Impressions:** times your pin showed. **Saves:** users saved it (intent + reach signal).
- **Pin clicks:** clicks on the pin itself. **Outbound clicks:** clicks through to your blog (the one that earns).
- **Outbound CTR** = outbound clicks ÷ impressions. Low CTR with high impressions → the pin design/hook needs work.

## Actions
- Best window check: when do YOUR pins get engagement? Adjust scheduling.
- Retire/redesign pins with impressions but no saves after ~30 days.
- Boards gaining traction → make more pins for that topic; weak boards → re-evaluate.
