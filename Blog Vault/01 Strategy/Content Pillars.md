## Pillar detail
### 1. Balcony Decor & Styling — the Pinterest magnet
Clusters: small balcony decor, apartment balcony decorating, styles (boho/modern/cozy/Mediterranean/Scandi),
makeovers/before-after, color themes. **This is the live cluster** ([[Content Tracker]]).

### 2. Balcony Furniture & Seating — highest revenue
Clusters: best small-balcony furniture, bistro sets, balcony chairs, swing/hanging chairs (12.7k), space-saving
foldables, storage benches, small sofas, bar tables + stools, railing-mount tables.

### 3. Balcony Privacy & Shade — concrete buyer intent
Clusters: privacy screens, outdoor curtains (10k), blinds, privacy plants, trellis, shade sails, umbrellas, pergolas.

### 4. Balcony Garden & Plants — durable, less-throttled
Clusters: balcony garden ideas, best plants by sun, vertical gardening, railing planters, herbs/veggies, container
gardening, privacy plants, low-light plants. *(Gardening is exempt from Pinterest's GenAI opt-out.)* ⚠ Plant-safety
claims must be verified (YMYL).

### 5. Balcony Flooring — functional buyer
Clusters: balcony tiles, deck tiles, outdoor rugs, artificial turf, flooring ideas.

### 6. Balcony Lighting — repeatable, aesthetic
Clusters: balcony string lights, solar lights, lanterns, fairy lights, café/festoon lights.

### Seasonal (publish 6–8 weeks ahead)
Christmas balcony decor (9.4k — biggest seasonal), fall decor, cozy winter (heaters/fire tables = high AOV), Halloween.

## Build sequence (recommended)
1. Finish **Pillar 1** high-value posts: Privacy → Boho → Modern.
2. Launch **Pillar 2 (Furniture)** + **Pillar 3 (Privacy/Shade)** — fastest path to revenue.
3. Bring **Pillar 4 (Garden)** online as the durability + seasonality hedge.
4. Layer in Flooring/Lighting/Seasonal as supporting clusters.
