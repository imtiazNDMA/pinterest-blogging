## Link hygiene rules
- Always disclose (FTC) above the first affiliate link.
- Link pins to the **blog post**, not raw affiliate URLs.
- Describe products qualitatively — **no prices, no "cheapest," no stock claims** (Amazon ToS).
- Use a link-management approach (plugin or naming convention) so you can swap/track links later.
- Re-check links quarterly for expiry/breakage ([[Monetization Tracker]] compliance audit).
